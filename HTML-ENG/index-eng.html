Freshmen Guide

First steps after enrollment
  • Student card (X-card): used for canteen, student service and as proof of status.
  • Library registration: The University Library is great for studying and research.
Where to eat?
  • Student canteens: most affordable and common option – subsidized meals.
Accommodation
  • Student dormitory: cheaper with rules, great location and community.
  • Private accommodation: more expensive but flexible. Mind the contract and utilities!
Transport
  • Buses in Zadar: free transport for students within the city.
  • Walking: most key locations are within a 20-minute walk.
College and studying
  • Merlin system: the official e-learning platform for all courses.
  • Tip: Don’t wait until the last minute for exams. Check your emails – all notices are there.

Studying? You have more than you think.

Enrolling in university opens a world of student benefits:

  • Subsidized meals in canteens
  • Accommodation in student dormitories
  • Free public transport within Zadar
  • Student service
  • Discounts on cultural events
  • Discounts for National Park tickets
  • Various discounts and services on the Kolegio platform
